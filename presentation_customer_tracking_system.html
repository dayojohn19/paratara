What is the Tourist Tracking System?

A comprehensive digital solution designed to monitor and manage tourist movements across multiple tourist spots in real-time.

Ensuring visitor safety, improving tourist experience, and providing valuable insights for destination management.

Key Features

📍 Real-time Check-ins

Track visitor locations as they check in at each tourist spot

👥 Live Visitor Count

Monitor active visitors at each location instantly

🚨 Safety Alerts

Automatic notifications for visitors staying over 24 hours

🧭 Tour Guide Tracking

Link tourists with their tour guides for better coordination

How It Works

1

Tourist Arrives

Visitor scans QR code or checks in at the tourist spot

2

System Records Visit

Time, location, and tourist details are logged automatically

3

Real-time Monitoring

Administrators can view all active visitors across locations

4

Safety Tracking

System alerts if visitors stay longer than expected

  • Data-driven insights for tourism planning
