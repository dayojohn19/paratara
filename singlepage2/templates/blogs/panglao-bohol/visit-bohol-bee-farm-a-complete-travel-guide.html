Discover the Sweet Serenity of Bohol Bee Farm

If you’re looking for a slice of paradise that offers both tranquility and adventure, Bohol Bee Farm in Panglao, Bohol, is your destination! Nestled on one of the most picturesque islands in the Philippines, this eco-friendly farm is not only a haven for organic farming and beekeeping, but it’s also a culinary delight, a cultural experience, and a beautiful escape into nature. Whether you're an adventurous traveler or simply seeking a peaceful retreat, Bohol Bee Farm has something for everyone.

How to Get There

Getting to Bohol Bee Farm is a journey filled with scenic views and the heartwarming hospitality of the Filipinos. To reach this delightful destination, you will need to first fly into Tagbilaran Airport, which is the main airport in Bohol. Flights operate frequently from major cities like Manila and Cebu, with ticket prices ranging from PHP 2,500 to PHP 4,500 one way, depending on how early you book.

Upon arrival at Tagbilaran Airport, you can choose from several transport options:

  • Taxis: The most direct option. A taxi ride to Panglao would cost around PHP 700-900.
  • Motorcycle Rentals: A budget-friendly option for the adventurous traveler. Expect to pay around PHP 300-500 per day.
  • Public Jeepneys: An authentic way to experience local transport, costing only PHP 15-30 per trip, but may take longer due to multiple stops.

Once you reach Panglao, Bohol Bee Farm is located just about 15-20 minutes away from the airport, making it easily accessible.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Bohol Bee Farm is during the dry season, which runs from November to May. These months boast sunny skies and pleasant temperatures, making it ideal for outdoor activities like tours, beach outings, and of course, exploring the farm. However, keep in mind that peak tourist season can lead to larger crowds around Christmas and Holy Week, so planning your visit slightly outside these dates could provide a more peaceful experience.

What to Do

Bohol Bee Farm offers a variety of activities that all ages will enjoy!

  • Farm Tours: Learn about organic farming and beekeeping. Guided tours help you understand the important role bees play in our ecosystem.
  • Farm-to-Table Dining: Savor delicious meals prepared with organic ingredients from the farm. Don’t miss their famous malunggay (moringa) pesto!
  • Classes and Workshops: Participate in cooking classes and workshops focusing on organic farming practices, making it a hands-on experience you won’t forget.
  • Shopping: Visit the farm's store for unique organic products, bee-inspired souvenirs, and honey products.
  • Massage and Spa Services: Treat yourself to a relaxing massage with soothing views of the ocean.
  • Photography: Capture the beauty of the farm with stunning coastal views and vibrant flora!

Where to Stay

While Bohol Bee Farm is an attraction in itself, it's also surrounded by various accommodation options that cater to different budgets:

  • Bohol Bee Farm Resort: If you want to immerse yourself completely in the environment, this is the ideal choice. Stay in charming accommodations that reflect the farm's eco-friendly ethos.
  • Fernandina Beach Resort: Just a few minutes away, offering beachfront views and cozy rooms.
  • Alona Beach Resorts: From luxury to budget, Alona Beach has a variety of options that suit different travelers’ needs.

Booking in advance is recommended, especially during the high season, to secure the best accommodations.

Local Tips & Recommendations

  • Timing is everything. Try to arrive right when the farm opens to enjoy a quieter atmosphere.
  • Bring a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated as you explore.
  • Wear sunscreen and insect repellent; these will greatly improve your comfort level!
  • Don't forget your camera! Each corner of the farm provides a stunning backdrop for your photos.

Budget & Costs

As with any trip, budgeting is crucial. Here’s a straightforward guide to costs you might expect:

  • Flight Cost: PHP 5,000 - PHP 9,000 round trip, depending on your starting point.
  • Transportation: PHP 700-900 for taxi rides to and from the farm.
  • Farm Entry and Tours: Entry is often inclusive, with activities and tours ranging from PHP 100 to PHP 500 depending on the depth of the experience.
  • Dining: Expect to spend PHP 300-800 per meal.

Overall, budgeting around PHP 2,500 - PHP 5,000 per day for a comfortable experience is advisable.

Conclusion: Your Sweet Escape Awaits

Bohol Bee Farm in Panglao offers a unique blend of adventure, tranquility, and sustainability. The stunning landscape, delicious organic food, and the remarkable work of bees create a memorable experience that leaves a lasting impression. Allow this delightful destination to inspire you to appreciate nature, savor fresh flavors, and enjoy life’s simple pleasures. Pack your bags, and make your way to Bohol Bee Farm – your sweet escape awaits!
