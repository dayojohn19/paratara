Discover the Natural Beauty of Capilay Spring Park, Siquijor

Nestled within the enchanting island of Siquijor, Capilay Spring Park stands as a serene oasis amidst lush greenery and bubbling waters. Famous among both locals and travelers, this park offers visitors an opportunity to immerse themselves in the island’s natural beauty, unwind in its clear waters, and soak up the tranquil atmosphere. Whether you're seeking a cool dip, captivating scenery, or a relaxing picnic spot, Capilay Spring Park has something special to offer for everyone.

How to Get There

Reaching Capilay Spring Park is relatively straightforward, making it accessible for day-trippers from the nearby attractions on Siquijor. If you’re coming from Siquijor’s capital, Larena, you'll find the park located approximately 10 kilometers away in the town of San Juan.

Transport Options and Costs:

  • Tricycles: The most common mode of transport on Siquijor. A tricycle ride from Larena to Capilay Spring Park typically costs around PHP 200-300 one way. It’s best to negotiate your fare before beginning the journey.
  • Motorbike Rentals: If you're feeling adventurous, consider renting a motorbike for around PHP 300-500 a day. Riding around the island is a thrilling experience, and you can easily navigate to the park and other attractions.
  • Buses: While there are limited options, some buses may operate between the towns, with minor stops. Bus fares are usually very affordable, around PHP 50-100, depending on the route.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to explore Capilay Spring Park is during the dry season, between November and May. During these months, the weather is generally dry and sunny, providing excellent conditions for outdoor activities.

Despite this, the park can get busier during peak tourist seasons like Christmas and Holy Week, so if you prefer more solitude, plan your visit during the shoulder months of May and October for a quieter experience.

What to Do

Capilay Spring Park is a multifaceted destination, offering a variety of activities that cater to different interests:

  • Swimming and Relaxing: The clear spring waters are inviting. Visitors often take a refreshing swim. The pools have different depths, making it suitable for families and solo travelers alike.
  • P picnic Areas: Pack a delicious lunch and take advantage of the well-maintained picnic spots under the shade of lush trees. It's the perfect way to enjoy the scenery and local flavors.
  • Exploring the Surrounding Nature: Take a stroll around the park and enjoy the well-kept gardens. The tranquil setting is ideal for photography enthusiasts or simply relaxing with a good book.
  • Community Vibe: Engage with the locals, many of whom visit the park frequently for leisure and social gatherings.

Where to Stay

If you're planning to spend more than a day enjoying Siquijor's beauty, consider several nearby accommodation options:

  • Casa de la Playa: Located near the beach, this cozy guesthouse offers comfortable rooms and friendly service.
  • Balay Ni Mayang: A quaint and affordable homestay option with easy access to Capilay Spring Park.
  • Shanti Beach Resort: More upscale, providing beach access along with restaurant services and serene ambiance.

Local Tips & Recommendations

Highlights to Enhance Your Visit:

  • Bring your own refreshments and snacks to enjoy at the picnic area; there aren’t many food stalls inside the park.
  • Don’t forget your camera — the setting is picturesque!
  • Visit early in the morning for fewer crowds and a more tranquil atmosphere.
  • Pack reef shoes if you plan to walk around the spring, as the stones can sometimes be slippery.

Budget & Costs

Visiting Capilay Spring Park is budget-friendly. Here’s a quick breakdown of expected costs:

  • Transport: PHP 200-300 (one way - tricycle), PHP 300-500 (motorbike rental per day)
  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Food & Drinks: Budget around PHP 200-500 for meals and refreshments, depending on where you choose to eat.
  • Total Day Budget: Approximately PHP 700-1,300 (including transport, food, and any activity).

Embracing Siquijor's Charm

As you journey through Capilay Spring Park and its surroundings, take a moment to embrace the island's tranquil vibe. Siquijor is not solely about the attractions—it's about the experience, the warm islander hospitality, and moments of peace in nature. Let the rhythmic flow of the springs wash away your stress and allow the beauty of Siquijor to captivate your spirit.

Your Adventure Awaits in Siquijor

Capilay Spring Park is a snapshot of Siquijor’s natural beauty and a must-visit for anyone traveling to the island. Whether you're soaking in the spring waters, enjoying a delicious picnic, or simply taking in the tranquil surroundings, this destination offers a welcome retreat for your mind and body. So pack your bags, grab your friends or family, and make your way to this beautiful hidden gem!
