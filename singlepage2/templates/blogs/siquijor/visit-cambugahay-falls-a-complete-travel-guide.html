Discover the Natural Beauty of Cambugahay Falls in Siquijor

Hidden within the lush jungles of the enchanting island of Siquijor, Cambugahay Falls exudes a magical allure that captivates all who step into its crystal-clear waters. This trio of tiered waterfalls is not only a postcard-perfect destination but also an ideal spot for adventurous souls seeking relaxation and natural beauty. Whether you’re a thrill-seeker looking to jump from majestic heights or someone seeking tranquility amidst nature, Cambugahay Falls offers a slice of paradise that feels like a dream come true.

How to Get There

Reaching Cambugahay Falls is relatively straightforward, as Siquijor is well-connected through various transport options. The island is accessible via ferries from Dumaguete, Cebu, and other surrounding islands. Once you set foot on Siquijor, here’s the detailed direction:

  • From the ferry terminal in Siquijor, rent a motorbike (recommended for the adventurous type) or take a tricycle to the falls.
  • The falls are approximately a 30-40 minute ride from the town of Siquijor.

Expect to pay around PHP 500 for a tricycle ride, while motorbike rentals typically range from PHP 250 to PHP 400 per day. Just a quick tip: be sure to fill your fuel tank as some areas en route can be sparse on gas stations.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Cambugahay Falls is during the dry season, which lasts from November to April. This period offers perfect weather with minimal rainfall and clear skies, allowing visitors to fully enjoy the waterfalls and surrounding greenery. However, the waterfalls are fascinating to behold year-round, so if you don’t mind a little rain, visiting during the wet season can lead to fewer crowds and an invigorating natural experience.

What to Do

At Cambugahay Falls, there’s no shortage of activities to keep you entertained:

  • Swimming: The inviting turquoise waters are perfect for a refreshing dip.
  • Cliff Diving: If you're feeling brave, take the plunge from one of the higher ledges that rise dramatically above the falls.
  • Picnicking: Bring along some snacks and relax on the grassy areas while soaking in beautiful views.
  • Photography: Capture the dreamlike beauty of the falls, surrounded by verdant foliage.
  • Explore the Paths: A short hike in the surrounding area rewards you with stunning views and an opportunity to interact with local wildlife.

Where to Stay

Finding accommodation near Cambugahay Falls is easy, with options ranging from budget-friendly to mid-range. Here are some recommendations:

  • Casa de la Playa: A charming beachfront hotel located nearby, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventures.
  • J&D Guesthouse: An affordable lodging option that provides comfortable rooms and a homey atmosphere.
  • The Coco Grove Beach Resort: For those looking for a more luxurious experience, this resort offers upscale amenities and proximity to both the falls and the beach.

Local Tips & Recommendations

Local Highlights

  • Take an early morning trip to avoid crowds.
  • Bring your own food and drinks to enjoy a picnic at the falls.
  • Wear water shoes to protect your feet from sharp rocks while swimming.

Budget & Costs

Your overall budget for a day trip to Cambugahay Falls can vary based on transport and activities. Here’s a breakdown:

  • Transportation: PHP 250 - PHP 500 (motorbike rental or tricycle)
  • Food and drinks: PHP 250 - PHP 500 (depending on where you eat or your picnic spread)
  • Entrance Fee: Free
  • Activities: Free, unless you rent equipment.

A rough estimate for a day can be around PHP 500 to PHP 1,500 depending on your activities and spending habits.

Embrace Siquijor’s Spirit

Siquijor is known for its mystical folklore and healing practices. Take a moment to learn about the island’s local traditions and perhaps even embrace a few of the laid-back island vibes as you unwind at Cambugahay Falls. Appreciate peace and serenity, and don’t rush your experience.

Ready to Dive into Paradise?

Whether you’re seeking an adrenaline rush or a serene getaway, Cambugahay Falls in Siquijor has something for everyone. Plan your trip to experience this stunning natural wonder and immerse yourself in the local culture. Share your adventures and let the waterfalls create memories that will last a lifetime!
