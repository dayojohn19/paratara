Siargao Party Guide: Day by Day

Where to go and what to expect each night of the week.

Monday — Chill Start

Relaxed drinks, small crowd, good music

  • 📍 Where: El Lobo, Mama Coco, Happiness Beach Bar
  • 💡 Why go: Start the week easy, enjoy drinks by the beach
  • 🎯 Recommended for: Solo travelers, couples, anyone wanting a quiet night
  • ⏰ Best time to come: 6 PM – 10 PM
  • 📝 Tip: Come early, things end earlier on Mondays

Tuesday — Acoustic & Cocktails

Acoustic sets, cocktails, social crowd

  • 📍 Where: Bravo, White Beard Coffee, Lamari
  • 💡 Why go: Chill acoustic vibes, meet other travelers
  • 🎯 Recommended for: Travelers wanting to socialize, music lovers
  • ⏰ Best time to come: 7 PM – 11 PM
  • 📝 Tip: Perfect for socializing

Wednesday — Midweek Party

Dance, DJ, mixed locals & tourists

  • 📍 Where: El Lobo (DJ Night), Mama Coco (Late night)
  • 💡 Why go: Midweek boost, enjoy DJs and dancing
  • 🎯 Recommended for: Party seekers, people who like dancing
  • ⏰ Best time to come: 8 PM – 12 AM
  • 📝 Tip: One of the first real party nights of the week

Thursday — Ladies Night / Pre-Weekend

Busier night, pre-weekend energy

  • 📍 Where: RumBar, Happiness Beach Bar, Lamari
  • 💡 Why go: Great promos, themed nights, social crowd
  • 🎯 Recommended for: Groups of friends, ladies enjoying themed nights
  • ⏰ Best time to come: 8 PM – 12 AM
  • 📝 Tip: Expect themed parties

Friday — Full Party Night

Packed, DJs, dancing until late

  • 📍 Where: El Lobo, RumBar, Mama Coco
  • 💡 Why go: Classic full-party night, best energy of the week
  • 🎯 Recommended for: Party-goers, travelers looking for lively nights
  • ⏰ Best time to come: 9 PM – 2 AM
  • 📝 Tip: Arrive before 9 PM to avoid lines

Saturday — Biggest Night

Peak party night, international crowd

  • 📍 Where: El Lobo, RumBar, Harana, Two Monkeys / Two Brothers, After-parties around Tourism Road
  • 💡 Why go: Most lively night, DJs, dancing, and after-party hopping
  • 🎯 Recommended for: Big groups, night owls, party enthusiasts
  • ⏰ Best time to come: 9 PM – 3 AM
  • 📝 Tip: Best night to go all out; arrive early for tables

Sunday — Sunset & Wind Down

Sunset drinks, chill beats

  • 📍 Where: Bravo (Sunset), Happiness Beach Bar, Lamari
  • 💡 Why go: Relax, recover, enjoy beautiful sunsets
  • 🎯 Recommended for: Couples, friends wanting a chill night, sunset lovers
  • ⏰ Best time to come: 5 PM – 9 PM
  • 📝 Tip: Perfect recovery night after Saturday
