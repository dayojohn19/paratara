The Sinulog Festival in Siargao 2023 promises vibrant parades, cultural displays, and a sense of community celebration. Here's how you can enjoy it:

  1. Plan Your Visit: The festival takes place on the third Sunday of January. Make sure to book your accommodations early as the island gets crowded.
  2. Join the Parade: The main parade features colorful floats, traditional dances, and lively music. Get a good spot along the main route early to enjoy the festivities.
  3. Enjoy Local Cuisine: Sample local delicacies at food stalls. Don't miss trying lechon (roasted pig) and fresh seafood.
  4. Participate in Activities: Throughout the festival, partake in street parties, cultural shows, and competitions that highlight local talent.
  5. Stay Safe: Ensure you stay hydrated and be aware of your belongings in crowds.

Cost Breakdown

  • Transport: ₱500 - ₱1,000 for local transport
  • Food: ₱200 - ₱500 for local meals per day
  • Accommodation: ₱2,000 - ₱5,000 for one night
  • Festival Fare: Free entry to most events
