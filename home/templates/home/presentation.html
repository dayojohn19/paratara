Problem → Solution

Tourist destinations often rely on manual reporting and fragmented tools, making it hard to answer: “Who is where, right now?”

This platform digitizes visits with QR check‑ins, live dashboards, and tour guide linkage—improving safety response, coordination, and planning.

How the Website Works (Tourist Funnel)

Tourists usually start with broad research online, then need a simple, local companion once they arrive on the island.

1

Discovery (Wide Internet)

Users search blogs and travel guides about the island across the web.

2

On-Island Experience (Simple Website)

Once they arrive, they’re funneled into a simple site where they can quickly find: accommodation, transportation, foods, services, activities, events, and promotions.

How It Works (End‑to‑End)

1

Scan to Enter

Visitor scans a QR code to open a protected entry page and start a visit session.

2

Instant Visit Logging

System records time, place, and visit status for live monitoring and later analytics.

3

Monitor + Coordinate

Admins see live counts; visitors can be associated with tour guides for coordination.

4

Safety & Response

Extended stays and risky patterns can be flagged; historical movement helps respond faster.

Value Proposition (Why It Wins)

  • Improves visitor safety with live monitoring + visit history
